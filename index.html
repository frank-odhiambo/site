Welcome to my site.

I am a PhD student in Economics at the ETH Chair of Development Economics Group (DEC) specializing in economic development.
I have worked with Innovations for Poverty Action in Kenya for 5 years as a Research and Policy Manager, overseeing several research studies including projects on youth employment, tax compliance, public participation, consumer protection, financial contracts, women empowerment & entrepreneurship and; sanitation. I have also worked with Concern Worldwide on an operational study looking at urban resilience, and with Sanergy on sanitation in informal settlements. I hold a MA in Management from the University of Nairobi (Kenya) and a BSc in International Relations and Diplomacy from Masinde Muliro University of Science and Technology (Kenya). My broad interests are in development issues, education, poverty and inequality.
